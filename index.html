What are Lost Tarts?

Have you ever been to an estate or garage sale and seen a macrame wall divider or a pair of midcentury modern gravel art pieces? What about string art or typewriter art? Where are all of those other funky things that were made in the den in lieu of playing Parchesi?

Lost Tarts is dedicated to the resurgence of lost arts and crafts that have been tarted up. We sell new kits that reflect current times and refurbish old ones and tart them up. This is not the art your Aunt Agnes made zoned out on Valium.
